Receipe for the best Fudge Brownies!

Ingredients:

  • 1 box Brownie mix
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

    2. Grease a 9 x 13 baking dish.

    3. Combine the melted butter and sugar in a large bowl. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each, until thoroughly blended.

    4. Sift flour into bowl, along with brownie mix and mix until well blended.

    5. Bake in preheated oven until an inserted toothpick comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes. Remove, and cool pan on wire rack before cutting.

    6. Dust with powdered sugar and top with your favorite berries.

Enjoy our delicious brownies!
